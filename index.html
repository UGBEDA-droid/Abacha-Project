Ingredients

  • 300g of Abacha (dried shredded cassava)

  • 200ml of palm oil
  • 2 tablespoonful of pounded crayfish
  • 1 large (or two small) Onions
  • Red Chili pepper
  • Salt
  • 100g of finely chopped fresh Garden Egg leaves
  • Boiled Dried Fish or Stockfish
